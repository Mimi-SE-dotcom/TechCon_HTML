Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate developers and has since evolved into one of the premier technology conferences globally. Each year, it brings together thousands of innovators, engineers, and tech leaders to explore the latest in software, AI, cybersecurity, and more.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we strive to create a platform that inspires innovation, nurtures talent, and fosters collaboration. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through transformative technology experiences and knowledge sharing.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Lisa Huang

AI researcher and professor at MIT, Dr. Huang spoke at TechCon 2022 on ethical machine learning and the future of human-centered AI.

Marcus Lee

Founder of DevTools.io, Marcus is a serial entrepreneur and advocate for open-source development. His 2019 keynote on developer productivity was a highlight of the event.

Ananya Kapoor

Cybersecurity expert and author, Ananya shared critical insights on data protection strategies in an increasingly digital world at TechCon 2021.
